1. Run the updated v6-calculation-sheets-migration.sql once.

CALCULATION-SHEET SECURITY
- New administrator uploads default to Administrators only.
- The administrator may choose All approved users.
- The administrator may choose specific approved users.
- Non-admin users cannot list or download restricted sheets.
- The uploader and administrators retain access.
- Permissions are enforced by the server at listing and download time.
