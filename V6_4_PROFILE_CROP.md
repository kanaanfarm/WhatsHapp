# ConnectChat Pro Enterprise v6.4

## AI conversation controls

AI history remains private browser history, but its conversation controls are
now active:

- **Archive chat** hides or restores ConnectChat AI on the current account and
  device.
- **Delete all chat** clears the private AI history after confirmation.

Human conversation controls remain server-backed.

## Profile photo crop and zoom

Selecting **Upload photo** now opens an editor before upload:

- drag with a mouse or finger to position the image;
- zoom from the minimum cover size up to 3×;
- see the exact circular profile framing;
- upload a normalized 640 × 640 JPEG crop.

The square cropped result is stored centrally and delivered through the
authenticated avatar endpoint, so every device displays the same framing.

Opening another user's profile also clears old upload/removal status messages
from the previously viewed profile.
