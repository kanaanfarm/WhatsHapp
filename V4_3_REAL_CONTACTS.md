# ConnectChat Pro v4.3 — Real Contacts Update

- Removed all demo/placeholder quick contacts.
- Quick contacts now come only from approved real user accounts returned by the server.
- Current user, AI assistant, and groups are excluded.
- Online users appear first, followed by most recent conversations.
- The quick-contact row is hidden when there are no other real users.
- Service-worker cache version was increased to force browsers to load the update.
